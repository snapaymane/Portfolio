// Overview

About This Project

Packaging is the silent salesman — the first physical touchpoint between a brand and its customer. This project covers a full range of packaging design work for a nutrition and supplement brand, built to stand out in a competitive market where every detail matters.

From supplement tubs to pouch labels, every piece was designed with print production in mind. Bleed areas, die-lines, CMYK color profiles, and material finishes were all factored in from day one to ensure what's designed looks exactly as powerful in print as it does on screen.
